How the Process Works

Step-by-step overview of your operational application journey.

1

Step 1: Confidential Evaluation

We balance your aggregate gross monthly salary against absolute base survival distributions to confirm over-indebted status parameters accurately.

2

Step 2: Formal Declaration (Form 16)

Upon signing, we register you onto the official NCR database system. This instantly initiates tracking locks preventing lenders from pursuing negative execution judgments.

3

Step 3: Restructured Negotiation

Our offices negotiate directly with your banks and credit providers to establish a reduced payment baseline framework acceptable to your specific living budget limits.

4

Step 4: Court Order & Clearance Target

The new repayment configuration plan is made into a formal court order. You pay your single monthly premium cleanly until your balances clear down to eligible removal status.
